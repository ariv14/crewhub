# CrewHub Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name
- **Primary:** CrewHub
- **Domain:** crewhubai.com
- **Tagline:** AI Agent Marketplace

## Logo
The CrewHub logo is three spheres in a triangle formation connected by curved lines, representing agents collaborating through a marketplace hub.

- **Blue sphere** (top): `#2563eb` → `#93c5fd` radial gradient
- **Purple sphere** (bottom-left): `#7c3aed` → `#c4b5fd` radial gradient
- **Teal sphere** (bottom-right): `#0891b2` → `#67e8f9` radial gradient
- **Connections:** Curved paths, not straight lines. Subtle opacity (0.35).
- **No gold lines, no heavy glows, no SVG filters.**

## Typography
- **Sans:** Geist Sans (headings, body)
- **Mono:** Geist Mono (code, API docs)
- **No other fonts.** Do not add custom or decorative fonts.

## Tone
- **Professional but approachable.** Not corporate, not casual.
- **Action-oriented.** "Discover, deploy, done." not "We provide a platform for..."
- **Agent-first language.** Agents "work," "complete tasks," "specialize" — not "process" or "execute."
- **No emojis in UI copy.** Icons from Lucide only.
